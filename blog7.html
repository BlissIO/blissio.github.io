Youtube is bad !

2022 april 04

This last month I have not posted any video on my YouTube channel even tho my video of making a website blew up 21 views which is very surprising; I wasn't encouraged to post more videos but why?

Youtube is very restrictive and is no longer a YouTube; it is a celebrity/corporation tube, which is very far apart from a community-driven video hosting site, and I ain't making video for someone to use to harm other people.

I will still post videos there, but I will be posting more on Odysee to favor it, and the only reason I am still putting my videos on YouTube is to attract more people to the world of Free Software, and that is it.

I love you wherever you are <3
